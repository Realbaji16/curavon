# Curavon Tester Instructions v1

Thank you for helping test Curavon. This is an **early test version** — your feedback shapes the product.

---

## Before You Start

- This is **not** a finished medical product and **not** medical care.
- Curavon helps you **organize health concerns** and suggest **one safer next step**.
- Curavon **does not diagnose**, **prescribe**, or **replace a clinician**.
- **Do not use Curavon for a real emergency.** If something feels severe, sudden, or unsafe, seek urgent or emergency help in the real world.
- You may use **general or fictional examples** if you prefer (e.g. “I feel tired after poor sleep” instead of personal details).
- **Avoid entering information** you are uncomfortable sharing.
- **You can stop at any time** — no pressure to continue.

---

## During the Test

The facilitator may ask you to try specific tasks. As you go:

- **Say what feels confusing** — labels, steps, or wording you do not understand.
- **Say what feels useful** — anything that helped you feel clearer or calmer.
- **Say whether the next action makes sense** — on Today and after Ask or Guides.
- **Say whether anything sounds too medical or unsafe** — wording that feels like a diagnosis or treatment advice.
- **Try the tasks** the facilitator gives you; think aloud if you can.

You are testing the **app experience**, not your health.

---

## After the Test

Please share honest feedback:

- What did you think Curavon **was for** when you finished?
- Did the **safety message** feel clear? Too scary? Too soft?
- Would you **trust** this app? Why or why not?
- **Would you use it again tomorrow?** What would need to change?

Your name can be replaced with a tester code in our notes. We use feedback to improve clarity and safety — not to give medical advice.
