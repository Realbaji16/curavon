# Curavon Tester Consent Script v1

**Purpose:** Facilitator read-aloud before a test session  
**Status:** Usability testing script only — **not a legal document**

---

## Script (Read to Tester)

> “Thanks for helping us test Curavon. This session is about **usability, clarity, and how safe the product feels** — not medical care.
>
> **Curavon does not diagnose or prescribe.** It helps organize concerns and suggest one safer next step. It is **not** a replacement for a clinician or emergency services.
>
> **Please do not enter real emergency situations** or deeply sensitive personal information during this test. You can use **fictional or general examples** — for example, ‘I’ve been tired and stressed’ instead of specific private details.
>
> In this version, your data is stored **locally on this device or browser** unless you export it or we clear it after the session.
>
> Your feedback — including quotes — may be used to **improve the product**. You can **stop at any time** with no penalty.
>
> Do you have any questions before we begin?”

## If Tester Raises a Real Urgent Concern

**Stop the product test.** Do not continue Ask, Guides, or planning tasks.

1. Acknowledge calmly: “That sounds important — let’s pause the app test.”
2. Direct to appropriate **real-world help** (local emergency number, clinician, or trusted support).
3. Do **not** interpret symptoms or give health advice.
4. Note the event in the feedback tracker as a **facilitator safety stop**, not a product diagnosis.

See `tester-safety-boundaries-v1.md` for full rules.
