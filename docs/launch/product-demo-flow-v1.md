# Curavon Product Demo Flow v1

**Target length:** 5–7 minutes  
**Audience:** Investors, clinicians (non-clinical demo), early testers  
**Tone:** Calm, clear, no medical claims

---

## Before You Start

- Use a **fresh or lightly used** demo account with at least one prior check-in (optional).
- Have **no API key** ready to show fallback, or key ready to show AI — pick one story.
- Reset urgent paths if demoing normal flow only.

---

## Demo Script

### 1. Open Curavon (30 sec)

**Say:** "Curavon helps you choose one safer next health step. It organizes your notes — it does not diagnose."

**Show:** Onboarding skip if already complete; land on **Today**.

---

### 2. Today (45 sec)

**Show:** Greeting, current pattern, **one primary next action** hero card.

**Say:** "Today gives you one clear step based on what you've shared — not a list of ten things."

**Optional:** Tap check-in if empty; complete one quick step.

---

### 3. Quick check-in (45 sec)

**Show:** Sleep, energy, stress, mood, symptoms — complete and save.

**Say:** "Check-ins feed Curavon's memory without turning into a medical record."

---

### 4. Ask a normal concern (90 sec)

**Show:** Ask tab → landing → start intake → e.g. "I've had headaches and poor sleep."

**Say:** "Ask walks you through a guided intake — like a calm triage organizer, not a chatbot doctor."

**Show:** Result with **one next safe step**, "organized — not a diagnosis" header.

**Say:** "AI can help organize when allowed, but safety rules and fallbacks always apply."

---

### 5. Guided flow (60 sec)

**Show:** Guides tab → pick "Something feels off" or mood flow → 2–3 runner steps → result.

**Say:** "Guides combine learning and structured questions to produce a safe summary and next step."

---

### 6. Follow-up (30 sec)

**Show:** Today follow-up card if due; tap **Helped**.

**Say:** "Curavon closes the loop — did the step help? That feeds the next recommendation."

---

### 7. Doctor Summary (60 sec)

**Show:** Open Doctor Summary overlay → saved items → **Generate summary** button.

**Say:** "This is for visit prep — your notes organized for a clinician, not a diagnosis."

**Show:** Footer disclaimer on summary.

---

### 8. Local data controls (45 sec)

**Show:** Profile → export health data → mention delete and sign out.

**Say:** "In this version, data stays on this device. You can export or delete anytime."

---

### 9. Safety boundary (30 sec)

**Say:** "If someone enters chest pain or trouble breathing, Curavon stops normal planning and shows urgent guidance only — no casual self-care step."

**Optional:** Do **not** live-demo urgent unless audience expects it; describe instead.

---

## Closing Line

"Curavon is a safety-aware next-best-action companion — one step, governed AI, local-first data, and doctor-ready notes when you need them."

---

## Demo Don'ts

- Do not say "AI doctor," "diagnosis," or "treatment"
- Do not claim cloud sync or cross-device backup
- Do not skip the non-diagnosis disclaimer
- Do not demo urgent path then continue to normal result

---

## Backup Paths

| Issue | Fallback |
|-------|----------|
| No API key | Show rule-based next step; mention fallback |
| Empty Today | Run check-in first |
| No follow-up due | Explain it appears after actions |
| Demo account locked | Sign out and create new local account |
