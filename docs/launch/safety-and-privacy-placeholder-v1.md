## What Curavon Is

Curavon is for **organization, reflection, and next-step support**. It helps you:

- Check in on how you feel
- Ask about a concern in plain language
- Choose one safer next health action
- Prepare notes for a clinician visit

## What Curavon Is Not

- Curavon **does not diagnose** conditions.
- Curavon **does not prescribe** medications or treatments.
- Curavon **does not replace** professional medical, mental health, or emergency care.
- Curavon is **not** an emergency service.

## Safety

If symptoms feel **severe, sudden, or unsafe**, seek **urgent care** or **local emergency support** now. Curavon may show a safety screen and will not suggest normal self-care for urgent concerns.

## Data in This Version

- In this version, your health data is **stored locally on this device**.
- Data is **not synced across devices** unless a future version explicitly adds that feature.
- You can **export** or **delete** your local health data from Profile at any time.
- Signing out clears your session on this device but **does not delete** health notes unless you choose to delete them.

## AI Use

- AI may help **organize** your notes or **reason about safe next steps** from limited context.
- AI is **governed** by safety rules, call limits, and fallbacks.
- AI may use **rule-based responses** when no API key is available or when safety rules block AI.
- Curavon does **not** store raw AI prompts or full model responses in your export.

## Your Controls

- **Sensitive Mode** — hide sensitive text on screen
- **Export health data** — download a JSON file of your records
- **Delete health data** — remove health records from this device
- **Delete local account** — remove session; optional health data deletion
